    Logistics and Administration Manager.

    (2016 - present)

    Emabitex is a textile screen printing company with over 20 years of experience in the market, specializing in advertising and promotional print outsourcing for both public and private sector clients. Its main clients include the Government of the City of Buenos Aires, the Municipality of La Matanza, and well-known brands such as Elemento Moda, as well as small and medium-sized businesses. The company also offers personalized workwear sales directly to end clients. Emabitex is recognized for its commitment to quality, honesty, and competitive pricing.

    As Logistics and Administration Manager, my role covers the full operational cycle: from coordinating production deliveries to managing billing, collections, and direct customer service, ensuring that each order meets the client's expectations on time and within agreed conditions.
