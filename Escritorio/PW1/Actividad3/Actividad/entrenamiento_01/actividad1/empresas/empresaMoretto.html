    Warehouse Operator and Delivery Driver.

    PepsiCo (2018 - 2020)

    Depósito Moretto operated as a distribution center for PepsiCo, one of the world's leading food and beverage companies. The depot handled the storage and daily distribution of PepsiCo products across an assigned zone in Greater Buenos Aires, managing high volumes of merchandise and serving a wide network of retail and commercial clients.

    As Warehouse Operator and Delivery Driver, I was responsible for order picking, forklift operation, route planning, and autonomous delivery management, including cash collection and on-site customer service.
